Github (Webstrates Mapping)

In the Cognitive Canvas project at the UC San Diego Design Lab we are building an experimental software application to support concept mapping and note-taking in the college classroom.Our approach offers users the best of both worlds : concept mapping on a screen, with sketching on paper. We are exploring the multitude of design challenges involved in navigating the physical/virtual divide when producing learning content.

In pursuit of seamless multi-device collaboration, we are leveraging affordances of the award winning webstrates system, which blurs the traditional distinction between applications and documents.

Research Aims

  1. To explore the mechanisms underlying the effectiveness of mind mapping in learning
  2. To explore the role of input media on learner behavior
  3. To explore novel paradigms for interacting with information
